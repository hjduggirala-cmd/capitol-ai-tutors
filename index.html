The Prompt

"give me dinner ideas"

"Here are some dinner ideas: 1. Spaghetti 2. Tacos 3. Stir-fry 4. Grilled chicken 5. Salad…"

→ Generic. Forgettable. You could've Googled this.

The Prompt

"I have chicken thighs, half a bag of spinach, and leftover rice. One kid won't eat anything spicy, and I have 25 minutes. Give me one dinner I can actually make tonight, with steps ordered so nothing sits cold."

"Perfect — here's a 25-minute garlic-butter chicken and spinach rice bowl. Start the chicken first since it needs the longest… While it cooks, warm the rice… Add the spinach in the last 2 minutes so it stays bright… Mild for the kids; add chili crisp to just your bowl at the end."

→ Specific. Usable. Made for your actual Tuesday.

The tool didn't change — the conversation did. Giving AI real context is a skill, and it's very learnable. That's what we do together, using your life, not canned examples.
